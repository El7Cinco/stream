LiveRadio.ie

//liveradio.ie/

LiveRadio.ie is a station directory from Irish radio enthusiasts. It's going pretty strong with over 4500 entries and a neat UI. It obviously also lists international stations.

It doesn't have an API in itself, but utilizes HTML5 microdata. And the station logos are utilized directly in ST2 as favicons.
